Te Papa Korero uses NFC technology to give you a way to interact with our exhibits.

In order to be able to use it, you need to ensure that NFC (Near Field Communication) is enabled on your device.
It is possible to check if your device has NFC functionality here. If you are unsure how to enable NFC on your device, please talk to one of our staff members.

How to use

When you see a Korero logo at an exhibit, simply hold your unlocked device within a few centimeters of the logo to scan. The device will take care of the rest.
